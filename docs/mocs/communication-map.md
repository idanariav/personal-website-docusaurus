# Communication

## Notes


Communication shapes every relationship, negotiation, and collaboration. This Map covers the landscape of how we interact with others — from foundational attention and empathy through to influence, honesty, collaboration, and the relational structures that enable or block effective exchange.

[Active Listening and Empathy](/notes/active-listening-and-empathy.md) addresses the prerequisite for all good communication: the active practice of attention, acknowledgment, and inquiry — and what breaks down when it is replaced by dismissal.

[Boundaries](/notes/boundaries.md) examines how linguistic and relational boundaries filter communication and behavior — how making expectations explicit clarifies relationships and reduces ambiguity.

[Collaborative Communication](/notes/collaborative-communication.md) covers communication oriented toward joint problem-solving and mutual benefit — cooperation, shared language, and the practices that bridge different perspectives.

[Honest Communication](/notes/honest-communication.md) explores how honest communication works in practice: from trust and vulnerability through proactive disclosure to genuine repair when communication fails.

[Persuasion and Influence](/notes/persuasion-and-influence.md) examines how communication frames, performs, and influences — through language, story, body, and signal.
