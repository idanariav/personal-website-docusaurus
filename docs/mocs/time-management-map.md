# Time Management

## Notes


Time management is the discipline of allocating finite time and attention toward what matters most, while preserving the capacity to handle the unexpected.

### Allocating Resources Wisely

[Resource Allocation and Prioritization](/notes/resource-allocation-and-prioritization.md) covers the structural side: how to distribute limited time and energy across competing demands. Building reserves — deliberate slack in the system — is not inefficiency but resilience: capacity held in reserve absorbs disruption without collapsing the whole.
