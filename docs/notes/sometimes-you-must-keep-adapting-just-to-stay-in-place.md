# Red Queen Syndrome

## Notes

### Claim

The red queen syndrome is when we constantly have to [adapt](/notes/our-beliefs-must-adapt-as-context-and-goals-change.md) just to stay in place.

### Explanation

For example, having [Capabilities are malleable through effort and learning](/notes/capabilities-are-malleable-through-effort-and-learning.md) won't necessarily improve your [Finding your unique strength creates sustainable advantage](/notes/finding-your-unique-strength-creates-sustainable-advantage.md) compared to others because they keep evolving and improving as well.

While you of course get better objectively, you don't necessarily improve in [comparison](/notes/comparing-ourselves-to-others-damages-wellbeing-and-progress.md) with others.
