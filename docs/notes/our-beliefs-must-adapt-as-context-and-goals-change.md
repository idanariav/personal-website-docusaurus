# Adaptability

## Notes

### Claim
Adaptability is the ability to change over time based on both [Actions and meaning depend on situational and environmental circumstances](/notes/actions-and-meaning-depend-on-situational-and-environmental-circumstances.md) and our [Clear direction matters more than speed of progress](/notes/clear-direction-matters-more-than-speed-of-progress.md). This means that every perception we hold is based on it's [relevance](/notes/truth-depends-on-context-and-the-observers-perspective.md) to where and who we are now, and not on an absolute truth that is true for all eternity.

### Explanation
This requires a process of [Introspection develops self-knowledge through internal examination](/notes/introspection-develops-self-knowledge-through-internal-examination.md) to know whether our beliefs, behaviors and perceptions are still the best solution for the problems we face [Truth and goodness are defined by practical usefulness](/notes/truth-and-goodness-are-defined-by-practical-usefulness.md), while also being [mindful](/notes/intentionality-is-conscious-presence-that-replaces-automatic-default-behavior.md) because without paying attention we would not notice whether we need to change and [how](/notes/types-of-change.md) so.

### Why it matters
Without adapting, we would likely experience great hardships and frustration. Like an animal with thick fur that now lives in a dessert, being too rigid is a recipe for disaster. We can't be separated from our environment, we are constantly [influenced](/notes/environmental-design-influences-behavior-through-choice-architecture.md) by it. Same with our [Social environment shapes behavior and identity through norms and conformity](/notes/social-environment-shapes-behavior-and-identity-through-norms-and-conformity.md), the people around us keep on changing (whether they are aware of it or not), so how could we avoid doing the same?

### Examples

### Supporters
I believe it is within our nature to adapt, this what separates us from other animals. We are imbued with [processes](/notes/we-grow-stronger-by-adapting-to-stress.md) that are meant to adapt our biology to the environment, even at the [genetic](/notes/genetic-switches.md) level. Similarly, our identity is [shaped](/notes/identity-is-shaped-by-and-shapes-our-social-interactions.md) by others as well. So all we have to do in order to adapt is [open](/notes/capabilities-are-malleable-through-effort-and-learning.md) ourselves for change, to accept that [Life is change](/notes/life-is-change.md), so we must change as well.

### Opposers
Adapting is not easy, as a default we tend to resist change. It is like trying to incorporate a part that is in constant motion into something static. We wish to [stay true](/notes/acting-in-alignment-with-your-values-is-authenticity.md) to who we are, but what does that mean if "who we are" continues to change?
