# Distillment

## Notes

### Claim

Distilling means to separate the [Noise obscures truth with external interference and confusion](/notes/noise-obscures-truth-with-external-interference-and-confusion.md) from the core essence of something. To be aware of the distinction between [Distinguishing appearance from authentic substance reveals hidden conflicts](/notes/distinguishing-appearance-from-authentic-substance-reveals-hidden-conflicts.md). By removing the noise, we almost always receive a [smaller](/notes/understanding-requires-breaking-things-to-their-smallest-parts.md) idea, which would also make it [easier](/notes/distilling-to-essentials-creates-clarity-and-actionability.md) to understand, and to [utilize](/notes/knowledge-applied-across-contexts-enables-learning-generalization.md) it in other cases [Content is independent from the medium that transmits it](/notes/content-is-independent-from-the-medium-that-transmits-it.md).

### Explanation

To distill an idea involves the following steps:
1. **Identification** - Identify the core message / idea within the content
2. **isolation** - Isolate the idea from the rest of the text, meaning that you need to add/subtract parts of that idea in order to make it a standalone idea, without needing to refer to the original text in order to understand it. [less is more](/notes/improvement-often-comes-from-removing-rather-than-adding.md)
3. **Process** - Write the idea in your own words, make it more approachable to you. [Writing](/notes/writing.md)

Distillment requires often a combination of the [Executive Attention Network](/notes/executive-attention-network.md) and our [Relaxation activates inward thinking for creative problem-solving](/notes/relaxation-activates-inward-thinking-for-creative-problem-solving.md). The first allows us to understand the idea, to criticize it, while the second allows to make it more abstract, to see "the soul" of the idea.
