# F-it effect

## Notes


### Claim

When a [streak](/notes/momentum-is-movement-that-builds-self-reinforcing-forward-action.md) is lost or a [rule](/notes/absolute-rules-trade-flexibility-for-willpower-savings.md) is broken, there's no meaning to it anymore, so we lose all motivation and all restraints.

### Examples

For example, let's say that you had a "no meat" diet that you kept for 100 days. Either by mistake or a moment of weakness, you consume a tiny bite of meat.
