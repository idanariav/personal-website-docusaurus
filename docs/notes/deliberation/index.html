Deliberation

Notes​

Deliberation is the act of sharing Perspective with one another, it is to bridge the gap created by the Theory of Mind, and allow us to see through each other's eyes. A deliberation is not necessarily a tool for unifying our perceptions into one, but it is much more likely to reach an understanding, encourage Cooperation and find Win win situations. through deliberation while assuming

When we talk instead of argue, it is easier go pass the Stereotypes. However, it is not always easy to do in the right way. First we have to assume Neutrality, although it's natural to prefer our side over theirs, there will be no agreement if we believe we hold all the moral righteousness and that we are always right. We have to fill ourselves with Curiosity, to really try to understand the other side, and come with more questions than answers.

Communicating in such a way is invigorating, it's in our nature to communicate with others, to help and rely on others for our wellbeing and coordinating our behavior in our lives.
