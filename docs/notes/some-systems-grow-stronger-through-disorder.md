# Anti-Fragility

## Notes


### Claim

At the basic level, anti-fragility is a form of robustness, of the ability to withstand and resist external shocks, errors and randomness.

### Explanation

Anti-Fragility systems response well to [Randomness](/notes/randomness.md). Usually randomness can cause damage to a system, since most are built on [Optimization finds the efficient point balancing outputs and inputs](/notes/optimization-finds-the-efficient-point-balancing-outputs-and-inputs.md) that are counter productive to stability. However, smart system can expect that the worst will happen, so we need to be ready for it [Pre-mortem anticipates failure modes to enable preparation](/notes/pre-mortem-anticipates-failure-modes-to-enable-preparation.md).

Ignoring or even worse trying to reduce randomness can make us ignore important information for our stability and growth, similar to how disabling the price system has toppled communist economies, or just imagine how evolution would look like without biological mutations. [Our beliefs must adapt as context and goals change](/notes/our-beliefs-must-adapt-as-context-and-goals-change.md) is key, it allows us to adjust to the world around us, to be at [flow](/notes/living-in-harmony-with-natural-processes-enables-wellbeing.md) with it, to accept it's [signals](/notes/resonance-is-being-in-sync-with-reality-and-others.md), to improve the way we operate within it, instead of trying to live in [wishful](/notes/manifesting-imposes-beliefs-onto-reality-rather-than-accepting-it.md) thinking. We can't [predict](/notes/prediction.md) the future, nor are we capable of [rational](/notes/truth-is-discoverable-through-reason-and-logic-alone.md) our way into building perfectly stable systems. For our benefit in the long run, lessons have to be learned, and it has to be the hard way.

Through [hardships](/notes/struggle-is-an-inevitable-and-necessary-part-of-any-meaningful-growth-or-achievement.md) we learn and grow, and hardships usually comes in the form of [unexpected](/notes/uncertainty-creates-anxiety-when-knowledge-and-control-are-incomplete.md) challenges, or at least at a price, for example vaccinations and workouts both improve the body by making it suffer measured challenges [We grow stronger by adapting to stress](/notes/we-grow-stronger-by-adapting-to-stress.md).

### Why it matters
