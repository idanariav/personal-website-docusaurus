# Objectivism

## Notes


### Claim

Objectivism is the opposite of [Subjective perception filters how we see external reality](/notes/subjective-perception-filters-how-we-see-external-reality.md), it is to see things "as they are", to focus on facts and remove [Judgment adds subjective value to objective events](/notes/judgment-adds-subjective-value-to-objective-events.md) and [Narratives shape perception and identity by constructing subjective reality](/notes/narratives-shape-perception-and-identity-by-constructing-subjective-reality.md) from our life.

### Explanation

### Why it matters

Objectivism, if possible, provides us a way to [Zooming out gains perspective through mental distance](/notes/zooming-out-gains-perspective-through-mental-distance.md), to gain [distance](/notes/separating-actions-from-identity-allows-growth.md) from the world as we perceive it, and see how it really is. It's a tool that removes [Preferring personal benefits blinds us to others' perspectives](/notes/preferring-personal-benefits-blinds-us-to-others-perspectives.md), dismantle any sense of [Attachment distorts how we see the things we cling to](/notes/attachment-distorts-how-we-see-the-things-we-cling-to.md), and allow us to act more freely without being tied by narratives. A form of [Distilling separates essence from noise to simplify understanding](/notes/distilling-separates-essence-from-noise-to-simplify-understanding.md), of seeing the "true" core of something without to he added [Noise obscures truth with external interference and confusion](/notes/noise-obscures-truth-with-external-interference-and-confusion.md)

### Examples

The [Empirical testing not reason alone discovers truth](/notes/empirical-testing-not-reason-alone-discovers-truth.md) takes pride in it's objectivism, and arguably it has helped it uncover the [truth](/notes/truth.md) much better than other theories that are more subjective.
