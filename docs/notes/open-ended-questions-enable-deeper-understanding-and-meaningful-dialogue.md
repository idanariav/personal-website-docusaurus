# Open Ended Questions

## Notes


### Claim

Open ended questions are usually what allows to have meaningful answers, and perhaps even meaningful [Deep relationships require trust, listening, and empathy](/notes/deep-relationships-require-trust-listening-and-empathy.md).

### Explanation

These are not a simple "yes or no" question but rather questions that allow space for interpretation, for [Examining ideas rigorously before accepting them as true](/notes/examining-ideas-rigorously-before-accepting-them-as-true.md), and even [Self Reflection](/notes/introspection-develops-self-knowledge-through-internal-examination.md).

### Why it matters

We don't treat them as objects to be manipulated, rather as capable people who can come up with solutions on their own.

### Examples

### Supporters

This also opens up a channel for [Deliberation is a discussion that bridges different perspectives](/notes/deliberation-is-a-discussion-that-bridges-different-perspectives.md) by giving them a place to express their opinions rather than limit themselves to simplistic answers.

### Opposers

They open the mind, thus contrasting [Binary thinking reduces complexity to false dichotomies](/notes/binary-thinking-reduces-complexity-to-false-dichotomies.md).

To ask open ended questions is the opposite of trying to [micro-manage](/notes/micro-management-restricts-autonomy-and-inhibits-growth.md) someone.
