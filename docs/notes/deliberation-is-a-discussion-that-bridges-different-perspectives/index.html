Deliberation

Notes​

Claim​

Deliberation is the act of sharing Perspective with one another, it is to bridge the gap created by the Understanding others requires attributing mental states like beliefs and intentions to them, and allow us to see through each other's eyes.

Explanation​

A deliberation is not necessarily a tool for unifying our perceptions into one, but it is much more likely to reach an understanding, encourage Cooperation is working together toward shared mutual benefit and find Win-win outcomes align interests and benefit all parties. through deliberation while assuming

First we have to assume Neutrality, although it's natural to prefer our side over theirs, there will be no agreement if we believe we hold all the moral righteousness and that we are always right. We have to fill ourselves with Curiosity is the joy of filling knowledge gaps, to really try to understand the other side, and come with more questions than answers.

Why it matters​

Communicating in such a way is invigorating, it's in our nature to communicate with others, to help and rely on others for our wellbeing and coordinating our behavior in our lives.
