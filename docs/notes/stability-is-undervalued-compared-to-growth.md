# Stability

## Notes

### Claim

Stability is an undervauled trait, like [Consistency compounds results through reliable repeated action](/notes/consistency-compounds-results-through-reliable-repeated-action.md). Nowadays we are much more prone to "go big or go home", not noticing that we trade off stability for growth.

### Explanation

We over [optimize](/notes/optimization-finds-the-efficient-point-balancing-outputs-and-inputs.md) every aspect of our lives which makes them less robust. The smallest change make cause the collapse of the entire system.
