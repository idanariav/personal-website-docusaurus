# Momentum

## Notes


### Claim

Momentum is a matter of [Consistency compounds results through reliable repeated action](/notes/consistency-compounds-results-through-reliable-repeated-action.md) and [Timing](/notes/actions-yield-more-when-timed-to-context.md), to able to move forward not through the strength of determination but rather by a sense of [Flow is deep immersion where performance peaks and self-awareness disappears](/notes/flow-is-deep-immersion-where-performance-peaks-and-self-awareness-disappears.md).

### Explanation

It is a form of [One-time effort investment yields future benefits](/notes/one-time-effort-investment-yields-future-benefits.md), that by making a small initial investment, we can create a force that will propel us in the future without any additional effort [Repeated actions yield exponentially increasing returns over time](/notes/repeated-actions-yield-exponentially-increasing-returns-over-time.md). The key is to be [frictionless](/notes/friction-is-resistance-that-blocks-action-and-behavior-adoption.md) as possible.

### Why it matters

It seems that the best way to have momentum, is to lower [Expectations shape wellbeing through the gap between desires and reality](/notes/expectations-shape-wellbeing-through-the-gap-between-desires-and-reality.md) and [Starting small reduces resistance and builds momentum for change](/notes/starting-small-reduces-resistance-and-builds-momentum-for-change.md). Once the action we want to do is not a huge commitment as it seemed, and even just a few minutes is enough, it lowers the psychological barriers of doing it. And once we start the ball rolling, the momentum is generated by itself through the process of [Habituation](/notes/repeated-actions-become-automatic-through-habit-formation.md).

### Opposers

Momentum can unfortunately be negative.
