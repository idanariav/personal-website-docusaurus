# Safe Base

## Notes

### Claim

"Safe Base" means to have somewhere or [someone](/notes/deep-relationships-require-trust-listening-and-empathy.md) where we feel belong to. A place of support, of [Psychological safety enables risk-taking and vulnerability without fear of retribution](/notes/psychological-safety-enables-risk-taking-and-vulnerability-without-fear-of-retribution.md), a place to [recharge](/notes/rest-enables-recovery-and-sustained-performance.md), build up [Reserves build capacity to absorb unpredictability and disruption](/notes/reserves-build-capacity-to-absorb-unpredictability-and-disruption.md) and to let go of life's worries [Stress can be adaptive or harmful depending on perception and context](/notes/stress-can-be-adaptive-or-harmful-depending-on-perception-and-context.md).

### Supporters

A safe place is where we feel [validated](/notes/accepting-others-feelings-and-perspectives-without-dismissal.md), we feel seen, understood. We don't have to pretend, or to hide our "ugly" parts, we can just [be](/notes/acting-in-alignment-with-your-values-is-authenticity.md) ourselves. We can be [vulnerable](/notes/vulnerability-is-emotional-openness-enabling-authentic-connection.md). We [Trust enables reliance on others honesty and good intent](/notes/trust-enables-reliance-on-others-honesty-and-good-intent.md) those around us, we know that they desire our wellbeing, and would always be there for us.

Having a safe base allows us to [venture](/notes/standing-up-for-yourself-is-courageous-authenticity-against-social-pressure.md) out into the world, knowing we always have a place to go to if things get worse.
