# Inclusive neutrality seeks balance by representing all voices equally

## Notes

### Claim

Inclusive neutrality pursues balance by including all possible voices to achieve fair representation, rather than eliminating influences.

### Explanation

This form of neutrality is easier to pursue in the humanities, where exclusive neutrality is impossible since you can't exclude the person making the decision. Instead of removing forces, inclusive neutrality embraces [Diversity of perspectives strengthens decision-making and understanding](/notes/diversity-of-perspectives-strengthens-decision-making-and-understanding.md) to let competing viewpoints balance each other out.
