# Flow

## Notes


### Claim

Flow is described as the situation of a perfect immersed experienced, in which we forget ourselves and [Focus is concentrated cognitive effort that maximizes performance on a single task](/notes/focus-is-concentrated-cognitive-effort-that-maximizes-performance-on-a-single-task.md) solely on the task at hand, performing at [peak](/notes/optimization-finds-the-efficient-point-balancing-outputs-and-inputs.md) efficiency, and experiencing great joy from it [Unity is the felt sense of connection with others and the world](/notes/unity-is-the-felt-sense-of-connection-with-others-and-the-world.md).

### Explanation

We feel connected to it, as if it is part of who we are [Alienation means feeling detached from life and others](/notes/alienation-means-feeling-detached-from-life-and-others.md). Our mind [quiets](/notes/intentionality-is-conscious-presence-that-replaces-automatic-default-behavior.md) down and the task consumes the entirety of our thoughts (or we have no thoughts at all)

the flow experience is achieved when:
1. **the task is challenging** - on the borderline between boredom and anxiety (not too easy, not too hard). [Challenge](/notes/struggle-is-an-inevitable-and-necessary-part-of-any-meaningful-growth-or-achievement.md)
2. **The task has clear goals and visible feedback** [Feedback is information that enables behavioral improvement](/notes/feedback-is-information-that-enables-behavioral-improvement.md)
3. **We find purpose in the task** - We have [Intrinsic motivation drives action through internal alignment and passion](/notes/intrinsic-motivation-drives-action-through-internal-alignment-and-passion.md) to fulfill this task, we find purpose in it or see it as something that is an expression of ourselves.
4. **We have agency** - It is hard to be in flow in an experience that is pure [Luck is external randomness affecting outcomes beyond control](/notes/luck-is-external-randomness-affecting-outcomes-beyond-control.md), that we have no control over. Usually it involves at least some kind of [Agency is the ability to connect desires and actions](/notes/agency-is-the-ability-to-connect-desires-and-actions.md), that we have something to do, that we can affect it through our actions.
