# Just World Bias

## Notes


### Claim

The "Just world" Bias means to think that good things happens to us because we deserve it, and bad stuff because of bad [Luck is external randomness affecting outcomes beyond control](/notes/luck-is-external-randomness-affecting-outcomes-beyond-control.md). And vice versa to other people. [Subjective perception filters how we see external reality](/notes/subjective-perception-filters-how-we-see-external-reality.md)

### Explanation

When something bad happens to us, we usually tend to relate it to bad [Actions and meaning depend on situational and environmental circumstances](/notes/actions-and-meaning-depend-on-situational-and-environmental-circumstances.md), such as: i had a bad day, i didn't sleep/eat/drink enough.

When something bad happens to others, we relate it to their personality - "they are stupid/uncaring/careless..." We [Blame is attributing fault instead of owning what's in your control](/notes/blame-is-attributing-fault-instead-of-owning-whats-in-your-control.md) the person and don't consider to look for the causal factors the same way we do with ourselves.

We think that there must be a "just reason" that bad things happen to bad people, and good things to good people. [Karma 2bc](/notes/karma-2bc.md)
For the same reason, when something good happens to us its because of our abilities and skills, but when it happens to others its because of chance or underlying privileges.

### Why it matters

The more we recognize our faults and virtues in ourselves and in others equally, the more [Empathy means understanding and acting on another's perspective](/notes/empathy-means-understanding-and-acting-on-anothers-perspective.md) we will have.

Therefore, we should:
1. You should always hold  [Judgment adds subjective value to objective events](/notes/judgment-adds-subjective-value-to-objective-events.md) at the first time you hear something good/bad about someone, first gather information
2. next, analyze this information with a bit of grace, remember that bad circumstances can be involved
3. always try to imagine other people trying to be their best
