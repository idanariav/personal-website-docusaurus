# People First

## Notes

### Claim

People First means that the most important resource at our disposal as a company is our people, our human capital.

### Explanation

### Why it matters

To acknowledge that by having an organizational [Capabilities are malleable through effort and learning](/notes/capabilities-are-malleable-through-effort-and-learning.md), we can improve our employees capabilities, and by extension our company. This also means that by taking care of our employees wellbeing, such as through [Finding meaning in work increases fulfillment and resilience](/notes/finding-meaning-in-work-increases-fulfillment-and-resilience.md), [Psychological safety enables risk-taking and vulnerability without fear of retribution](/notes/psychological-safety-enables-risk-taking-and-vulnerability-without-fear-of-retribution.md), and giving a sense of [Ownership means taking active responsibility and control of your environment](/notes/ownership-means-taking-active-responsibility-and-control-of-your-environment.md), we increase the chances of creating an environment of [Intrinsic motivation drives action through internal alignment and passion](/notes/intrinsic-motivation-drives-action-through-internal-alignment-and-passion.md) where they will be much more productive at their job.
