# Individualism

## Notes

### Claim

Individualism is the focus on the individual as the smallest unity of [Moral worth](/notes/moral-worth.md) and of thinking capacity, as opposed to more social views that look at the community as the smallest unit.

### Explanation

Individualism somewhat rejects the notion of [Identity is shaped by and shapes our social interactions](/notes/identity-is-shaped-by-and-shapes-our-social-interactions.md), and claims that at least on some level, there is an independent core to each person.
