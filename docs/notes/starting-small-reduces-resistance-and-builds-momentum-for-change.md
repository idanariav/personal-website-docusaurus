# Start Small

## Notes

### Claim

Start small means that change is more likely to occur when we begin with small actions rather than life changing decisions.

### Explanation

Whether its to start a new business, diet, exercise regime, or working on a personal project. Instead of making huge promises that are both exhausting and difficult to keep in the long run, why not start small?

### Why it matters

Starting small sounds easy, but it often requires us to [say no](/notes/saying-no-protects-time-and-energy-for-what-matters-most.md) to bigger commitments that perhaps embody our goals more. After all, what is the point in working out for only 5 minutes when we know that true lose weight begins at 20? However, the point here is to [Focusing on process and consistency yields better results than obsessing over outcomes](/notes/focusing-on-process-and-consistency-yields-better-results-than-obsessing-over-outcomes.md), and once we start small, it will be easier later to do more. First we must be sure we are moving in the right direction, before we run at full speed.

### Examples

For example, writing for just 15 minutes a day means 7.5 hours a month! Imagine what you can do with a free full day of work each month.

### Supporters

Similar to the []], not only we create a [[Momentum is movement that builds self-reinforcing forward action](/notes/5-minute-rule.md) for change, we also enable ourselves an easier [transition](/notes/repeated-actions-become-automatic-through-habit-formation.md) into a more balanced lifestyle that won't cause us to [collapse](/notes/burnout-is-chronic-exhaustion-from-overwhelming-demands.md).

Starting small also gives us [Flexability](/notes/our-beliefs-must-adapt-as-context-and-goals-change.md), because it is much easier to change, adapt and modify a small commitment than a large one.

But even if we continue maintaining our small commitment without changing it, as long as we are [patient](/notes/patience-enables-persisting-through-delayed-gratification-and-growth.md), we would still be able to see meaningful results in the long run [Repeated actions yield exponentially increasing returns over time](/notes/repeated-actions-yield-exponentially-increasing-returns-over-time.md). It represents a belief that we can [grow](/notes/capabilities-are-malleable-through-effort-and-learning.md), one small step at a time.
