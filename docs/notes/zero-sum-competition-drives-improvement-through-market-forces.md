# Zero-sum competition drives improvement through market forces

## Notes

### Claim

Competition is when each person tries to gain something at the expense of another [Cooperation is working together toward shared mutual benefit](/notes/cooperation-is-working-together-toward-shared-mutual-benefit.md). This results from [Zero sum](/notes/binary-thinking-reduces-complexity-to-false-dichotomies.md) thinking — either I get something or they do, but not both. This is the core logic behind [Capitalism](/notes/selfish-incentives-can-produce-collective-prosperity.md), because competition forces us to improve and focus on our [Finding your unique strength creates sustainable advantage](/notes/finding-your-unique-strength-creates-sustainable-advantage.md), resulting in a richer economy.
