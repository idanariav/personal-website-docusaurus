# Apology

## Notes


### Claim

Often an apology should come with an intent to "repair". Not only to ask for forgiveness but to act in a way to restore [Trust enables reliance on others honesty and good intent](/notes/trust-enables-reliance-on-others-honesty-and-good-intent.md) in the relationship. It can be to fix what has been broken, to dedicate time and energy to build something together, or work on a solution.
