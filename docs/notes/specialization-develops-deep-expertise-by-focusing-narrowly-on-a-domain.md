# Specialization

## Notes

### Claim

Specialization is when a person chooses or is assigned a certain task and he learns to do that task specifically very well. Meaning, that he prioritizes a narrow and deep view rather than wide and shallow.

### Explanation

Usually, when you specialize at something, this becomes your [Finding your unique strength creates sustainable advantage](/notes/finding-your-unique-strength-creates-sustainable-advantage.md), but is also dependent on the type of competition around you. If you are specialized in a very common and easy-to-master field, then you will still be highly replaceable.
