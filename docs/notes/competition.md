# Competition

## Notes

Competition is when each person tries to gain something at the expense of another (Opposes:: [[Cooperation]]). This results from a (Supports:: [[Binary Thinking|Zero sum]]) thinking, that either I get something or they do, but not both. This is the core logic behind (Supports:: [[Capitalism]]), because allegedly competition brings the best of us, it forces us to improve, to focus on our (Supports:: [[Competitive Advantage]]), which results in a richer (in terms of purchasing power and alternatives) economy.
