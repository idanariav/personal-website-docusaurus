# Competition

## Notes

Competition describes scenarios where participants strive to gain something at the expense of others. It appears in two forms: as an economic mechanism where market rivalry drives collective improvement, and as a mindset where winning over others replaces personal growth.

### Economic Competition
Market forces create environments where [Zero-sum competition drives improvement through market forces](/notes/zero-sum-competition-drives-improvement-through-market-forces.md), pushing participants toward innovation and efficiency.

### Psychological Cost
On the individual level, a [Competitive mindset harms wellbeing by focusing on opponents](/notes/competitive-mindset-harms-wellbeing-by-focusing-on-opponents.md), shifting attention from personal growth to relative standing.
