# Nutrition

## Notes

### The Body's Foundation
[Nutrition is a foundational need that enables higher functioning](/notes/nutrition-is-a-foundational-need-that-enables-higher-functioning.md) — without adequate fuel, the brain and body cannot support higher-order goals. This places nutrition at the base of any personal development effort, a prerequisite before we can pursue learning, creativity, or self-actualization.

### Strategy Over Willpower
The challenge of eating well is less about knowledge and more about environment. [Healthy eating requires replacing temptations with better defaults](/notes/healthy-eating-requires-replacing-temptations-with-better-defaults.md) — rather than fighting cravings head-on, the real leverage comes from designing our surroundings so that healthy choices are the path of least resistance.
