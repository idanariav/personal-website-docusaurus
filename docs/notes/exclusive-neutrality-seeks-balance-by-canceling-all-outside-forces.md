# Exclusive neutrality seeks balance by canceling all outside forces

## Notes

### Claim

Exclusive neutrality pursues objectivity by attempting to close off all outside forces, creating a controlled vacuum free from interference.

### Explanation

This form of neutrality is most visible in the scientific method, where [experiments](/notes/experimentation-is-the-only-reliable-way-to-improve-and-discover-truth.md) aim to isolate variables by eliminating confounding influences. The goal is a "clean" environment where only the factors under study remain active.
