# Post-mortem

## Notes

### Claim

The process of post mortem means to analyze both the results of a project and the methods used to get those results, in order to improve and learn for future cases.

### Explanation

It requires to adapt a [scientific approach](/notes/empirical-testing-not-reason-alone-discovers-truth.md) to our learning process, knowing that failure is not necessarily a bad thing, but rather it's a sign, an information towards the correct path.

It's not only relevant for failed cases to help us learn what went [wrong](/notes/failure-is-a-necessary-part-of-learning-and-growth.md) and what to do better, but perhaps just as much to successful cases to understand what we did right and how can we replicate it.

### Why it Matters

It's important that everyone feels [safe](/notes/psychological-safety-enables-risk-taking-and-vulnerability-without-fear-of-retribution.md) enough to open up and share their point of view of what happened and why.

Note that positive results doesn't necessarily mean that we acted well, perhaps this is a result of pure [Luck is external randomness affecting outcomes beyond control](/notes/luck-is-external-randomness-affecting-outcomes-beyond-control.md).
