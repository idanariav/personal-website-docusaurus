# Quantifiable Self

## Notes

### Claim

Quantifiable self is perhaps an extreme, data driven version of [Introspection develops self-knowledge through internal examination](/notes/introspection-develops-self-knowledge-through-internal-examination.md).

### Explanation

It has a level of [objectivity](/notes/seeing-reality-without-subjective-bias-reduces-distortion.md) to it, which allows us to compare our behavior across time.

### Why it matters

As with other quantitative measurements, it's important to avoid the [Treating measurable metrics as complete truth hides importance](/notes/treating-measurable-metrics-as-complete-truth-hides-importance.md), that we treat what's measurable as important and the rest as not.
