# Forward Thinking

## Notes

### Claim

Whenever we face a [Challenge](/notes/struggle-is-an-inevitable-and-necessary-part-of-any-meaningful-growth-or-achievement.md), we can either dwell on the past, filling ourselves with [Blame is attributing fault instead of owning what's in your control](/notes/blame-is-attributing-fault-instead-of-owning-whats-in-your-control.md) or [We regret inaction more than action taken](/notes/we-regret-inaction-more-than-action-taken.md) for failing to meet our [Expectations shape wellbeing through the gap between desires and reality](/notes/expectations-shape-wellbeing-through-the-gap-between-desires-and-reality.md), or we can focus on the road ahead, what we can do now and how we can act better next time [Optimism is choosing to believe outcomes can improve through action](/notes/optimism-is-choosing-to-believe-outcomes-can-improve-through-action.md).
